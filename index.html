I, Dalinar Kholin, write this in hopes that this will be seen by all who wish to defend that which they love against the forces of evil. I'm the leader of an ancient group known as the Knights Radiant. We are ten distinct orders of men and women who swear powerful oaths and gain extraordinary abilities to defend our world from a rising threat called the God of Passion, or Odium and his army of powerful Singers, a super race unlike anything anyone has seen before. Odium seeks the destruction of Roshar and all Humankind. Our purpose as Knights Radiant is to stop them. Every order has its own purpose: protectors, scouts, healers, scholars, diplomats, and warriors alike, all united in the defense of Roshar, but all are bound by ideals of responsibility, honor, and improvement. In this record, you will learn what each order stands for, what qualities its members must embody, and how its strengths contribute to our survival. Some orders have grown strong; others are in dire need of new recruits. If you feel the desire to protect, to build, to lead, or simply to become better than you were before, then there may be a place for you among the Knights Radiant. Come and unite with us, for the good of all.
