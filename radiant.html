Windrunner: -Ideal: I will protect -Spren Companion: Honor Spren -Surges: Adhesion and Gravitation -Description: Windrunners use their surges/powers, manipulate gravity, to fly, and launch themselves through the air, using magical bonds to strengthen protections and defend those who cannot protect themselves.

Edgedancer: -Ideal: I will remember -Spren Companion: Cultivation Spren -Surges: Abrasion and Progression -Description: Agile healers who can heal others with magical energy and glide frictionlessly across any surface, moving with unmatched grace in battle.

Lightwweaver: -Ideal: I will speak my truth -Spren Companion: Cryptics -Surges: Illumination and Transformation -Description: Masters of illusion who shape light and sound into realistic images, using creativity and emotional insight to change hearts and inspire growth.

Skybreaker: -Ideal: I will seek justice -Spren Companion: High Spren -Surges: Gravitation and Division -Description: Radiants who bind themselves to a strict code or oath and use precise magical force to deliver justice and strike targets with unerring control, while manipulating gravitation to further their reach.

Stoneward: -Ideal: I will be there when I’m needed -Spren Companion: Peak Spren -Surges: Coheision and Tension -Description: Durable fighters who can strengthen their bodies and the ground around them, granting immense stamina, toughness, and battlefield staying power.

Elsecaller: -Ideal: I will reach my potential -Spren Companion: Ink Spren -Surges: Transformation and Transportation -Description: Deep thinkers who can step between the physical world and a realm of thought, allowing them to teleport, communicate, and manipulate reality with reason and focus.

Truthwatcher: -Ideal: I will seek truth -Spren Companion: Mist Spren -Surges: Progression and Illumination -Description: Radiants with the ability to heal and receive visions of hidden truths, letting them repair what is broken and understand things others cannot see.

Dustbringer: -Ideal: I will seek self mastery -Spren Companion: Ash Spren -Surges: Division and Abrasion -Description: Users who can ignite and intensify destructive energy, giving them the ability to burn, break, or erode physical materials with increasing power.

Willshaper: -Ideal: I will seek freedom -Spren Companion: Reachers -Surges: Transportation and Cohesion -Description: Freedom-seeking adventurers who can reshape solid barriers and manipulate the physical world around them to break restraints and open paths for others.
